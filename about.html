About Rusununguko High School

What to know

Rusununguko High School, located in Ruwa, Zimbabwe, was established on March 7, 1981. Initially founded to accommodate ex-combatants and refugees, the mixed government school has evolved into one of the top-ranking institutions in Mashonaland East. Our school holds historical significance, being rooted in the post-independence era as part of Zimbabwe‚Äôs Education with Production (ZIMFEP) initiative. The school's philosophy integrates education with production, promoting the dignity of labor and hands-on learning experiences. Beyond academics, Rusununguko High School promotes a philosophy of education that integrates physical training and production, mirroring educational practices in refugee camps during its founding era. This approach ensures that students are not only academically competent but also skilled in practical and vocational areas.

Headmaster's Statement

As Headmaster of Rusununguko High School, I am proud to lead an institution that values both academic excellence and holistic development. Our commitment to the Education with Production philosophy ensures that students are equipped with practical skills and a strong work ethic, preparing them for success beyond the classroom. We celebrate our rich history and the achievements of our learners, and we remain dedicated to fostering an environment where every student can thrive and reach their full potential.

Pass Rates

Rusununguko High School 2024 A-Level Results Rusununguko High School achieved an extraordinary 99.1% pass rate, with 112 out of 113 candidates passing their A-Level examinations. Below is a detailed analysis of the results, including the highest performer and the distribution of points

In 2023, Rusununguko High School achieved impressive pass rates, reflecting its commitment to academic excellence and student preparedness. The school recorded a pass rate of 98% in A-Level examinations, showcasing exceptional performance, particularly in the sciences. This high achievement highlights the school's strong focus on science education, which has consistently contributed to student success in national assessments. For the O-Level examinations, Rusununguko High School reported a pass rate of 85%, demonstrating robust performance across various subjects. This level of achievement indicates a well-rounded educational approach, allowing students to excel in multiple disciplines. In 2023, Rusununguko ZIMFEP High School showcased impressive academic performance, highlighted by outstanding results in both O-Level and A-Level examinations. O-Level Achievements: Top performers included Chidziwa Panashe with 14 As and 8 Bs, and Mlala Nyasha with 9 As and 2 Bs. A-Level Success: Notable A-level students achieved high points, such as Kakora Kudakwashe with 20 points, and several others scoring 15 to 18 points.
