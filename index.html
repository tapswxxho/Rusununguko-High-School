~SUNUNGUKA

Welcome to Rusununguko High School, a beacon of academic excellence and holistic development in Ruwa, Zimbabwe. Established in 1981, our institution was founded with a vision to empower ex-combatants and refugees, and has since grown into one of Mashonaland East’s top-ranking schools. At Rusununguko, we believe that education is the key to unlocking potential and shaping future leaders. Our dedicated staff and vibrant student community work together to create an environment where discipline, ingenuity, and a passion for learning thrive. We offer a comprehensive curriculum aligned with the Zimbabwe School Examinations Council (ZIMSEC) standards, preparing students for both Ordinary Level (O-Level) and Advanced Level (A-Level) examinations. Our academic programs are complemented by a wide range of technical, practical, and extracurricular activities, ensuring that every learner receives a well-rounded education. From state-of-the-art science laboratories to dynamic sports teams and cultural clubs, Rusununguko High School provides opportunities for students to explore their interests and develop essential life skills. We invite you to discover more about our rich history, outstanding achievements, and the supportive community that makes Rusununguko High School a place where every student can shine. Join us as we continue to inspire, educate, and empower the next generation.
