What we offer

Curriculum Overview

Rusununguko High School offers a curriculum that adheres to the Zimbabwe School Examinations Council (ZIMSEC) guidelines, ensuring that students are prepared for both Ordinary Level (O-Level) and Advanced Level (A-Level) examinations. The curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive education that equips students with essential knowledge and skills.

Subjects Offered:

Core Subjects: The school includes Mathematics, English Language and Literature, and Sciences (Biology, Chemistry, Physics) as foundational subjects, which are mandatory for O-Level. Technical and Practical Subjects: Students can also choose from subjects like Agriculture, Woodwork, Metal Work, Fashion and Fabrics, and Home Economics. These subjects aim to enhance practical skills alongside theoretical knowledge.
