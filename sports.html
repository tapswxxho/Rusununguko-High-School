SPORTS AT RUSUNUNGUKO HIGH SCHOOL
Building Character, Teamwork, and Excellence

Our Sporting Culture

At Rusununguko High School, sports are an integral part of our holistic education approach. We believe in nurturing not only academic excellence but also physical fitness, teamwork, and resilience. Our students participate in a wide range of sporting disciplines, competing at district, provincial, and national levels.

Major Sports Offered

  • Soccer (Football): Both boys and girls teams compete in local and regional tournaments. Our boys' team recently won the Mashonaland East Schools Trophy in May 2025, defeating Goromonzi FC in a thrilling final.
  • Netball: The girls' netball team has consistently reached the provincial finals, with a memorable victory in 2023 and a close runner-up finish in 2024.
  • Athletics: Our athletes excel in track and field events, with several students qualifying for the National Youth Games each year. In 2024, two of our sprinters brought home silver medals in the 200m and 400m events.
  • Volleyball: Both junior and senior teams participate in inter-school leagues, showing steady improvement and strong sportsmanship.
  • Basketball: A growing sport at our school, with the boys' team reaching the district semi-finals for the first time in 2025.
  • Cricket & Tennis: Offered as part of our extracurricular program, encouraging skill development and recreational play.

  • Despite strong performances, our basketball team narrowly missed the finals in 2025, motivating us to invest in better training facilities and coaching.

  • Weather disruptions in 2023 affected athletics training, but our students adapted and still achieved notable results.

Looking Ahead

We are committed to further developing our sports programs, upgrading facilities, and fostering a spirit of healthy competition. Rusununguko High School continues to inspire students to strive for excellence both on and off the field.
